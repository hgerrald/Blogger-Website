What if God told you today that he has huge plans for you tomorrow? That he wanted to do something so incredible in your life, with your life, or use you in such a way that you immediately dropped to your knees because you knew how unworthy you are. What if He is molding you today, putting you through hell that others are not, so that you can do tomorrow what they can’t?

“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future”- Jeremiah 29:11

Now what if he told you that this season of preparation must first last one year for it to come to fruition? Would you still persevere for the Lord? Would you still finish the good race? Would you let the hand of our almighty father and creator mold you as he wishes or would you follow your own desires, those that Paul calls the desires of the flesh?

Consider it pure joy, my brothers and sisters, whenever you face trials of many kinds, because you know that the testing of your faith produces perseverance. Let perseverance finish its work so that you may be mature and complete, not lacking anything. - James 1:2-4

My call today is to finish the good race. I have no idea what’s waiting for me at the finish line. A couple months ago God gave me a taste of His plan and the result brought me to tears. I can only imagine how now, after persevering in this manner, knowing how difficult the journey has been, how sweet the taste of perfect love and pure rejoicing directed from my father God will be at the end of this season.
